Lindsay Danger

Dear Danger is Fresno's first mobile fashion boutique. A unique novelty shop with hand picked clothing brands and accessories. Our mission is to provide a carefully selected assortment of clothing brands and vintage apparel for every gal to enjoy. We dedicate our business to seeking new trends in retail by searching high and low thru blogs, magazines and events to provide the most coveted merchandise. Dear Danger can be found at festivals and events throughout California and is also available for privately hosted parties.
